fix(fb): Improve discussion tab detection

- Enhanced the `clickDiscus` method in `iv_fb.class.js` to be more robust.
- It now searches for multiple text variants ('Diskuze', 'Discussion', 'Hlavní', 'Featured') to handle different group layouts.
- The underlying `findByText` function in `iv_fb_support.js` was also updated to search within `<span>` elements in addition to buttons and links.
- This change prevents the bot from getting stuck when the primary "post" field is not immediately visible and a switch to the discussion tab is required.
