fix(logic): Overhaul group interaction logic

- Completely refactored the `handleSingleUtioPost` function in `iv_actions.js` to follow a new, more robust decision tree: analyze first, then act.
- The system now performs a full page analysis upon entering a group and decides the next step based on the analysis result (e.g., post, join, switch to discussion, or block the group).
- The `findByText` function in `iv_fb_support.js` was generalized to search for text in any element (`*`), removing the restriction to specific tag types, which makes it more flexible.
- The `_verifyGroupPosting` method in `iv_page_analyzer.class.js` was enhanced to detect more variants of the "Discussion" tab.
- This new logic prevents the bot from getting stuck, correctly identifies when a group requires joining, and handles cases where switching to a discussion tab is necessary before posting.
